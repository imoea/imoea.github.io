2025-05-06

On creative thinking:

  1. Discover something completely new. Seek things that the world has never seen but would be nice to have.
  2. Find hints in all sorts of places. There are inspirations that spark new ideas all around you just waiting to be found.
  3. Nurturing an idea. An invention isn't for just one person; have everyone use it.
  4. Look at things from every angle. Investigate every perspective.
  5. Don't just go with the status quo. Think over what you think are usual.
  6. Never give up. Even if you fail the first time and the second time, keep on trying.

— Momofuku Ando's creative process, from CUPNOODLES MUSEUM (YOKOHAMA).

2025-04-30

On how to become a good leader:

  1. Don't dwell on past decisions. Always look forwards.
  2. Don't be afraid of change. They are opportunities for you to grow.
  3. To acquire a breadth of understanding, read about everything. Everything is adjacent to something. Collect enough dots, and you will soon learn to connect them.

— Takeaways from CEO's dialogue at the Young Leaders Course in DSO.
