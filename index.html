2014-05-25

Each ear a pearl,
red hair a curl.
Here sits a girl,
her thoughts a whirl.

In much she's keen,
far out she's been.
Whole worlds she's seen,
through deep eyes green.

Of ashen hue,
an atlas blue.
Its seams unglue,
fables be true.

Along she feels,
valleys and hills.
Romance she steals;
adventure, thrills.

A princess fair.
A witch's dare.
A dragon's lair.
Hero, beware.

Whilst out of sight,
none sees the blight.
Left out of light,
she walks the night.
